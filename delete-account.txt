How to Delete Your Account
If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please email us at scilydestudios@gmail.com.

	When you request account deletion:
		•	Your account data, including login credentials and order history, will be deleted.
	•	Certain data (e.g., transaction records) may be retained for legal or business purposes, as required by Shopify’s terms and conditions.
	•	The process may take up to 7 days to complete.
